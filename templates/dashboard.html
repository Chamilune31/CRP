Forecast Health Overview

A single page to check whether the forecasts can be validated, or whether some items / months need a manual review first.

How to read this dashboard - a 5-step walkthrough

1
Start with the colored tiles below. They give you the headline numbers in one glance: how many items were revised, how many rows look inconsistent, and how many were flagged by each detector. If the warning-coloured tiles look high, something needs review.
2
Check the monthly trend chart. It shows when the problems happened. Spikes on the orange or red lines tell you which months to investigate first.
3
Look at the country bar chart. Countries on the left have the most inconsistencies - focus your audit there before everywhere else.
4
Scan the two Top-10 tables. "Most-revised items" tells you which products had the biggest forecast changes. "Suspicious items" blends all three detectors into one ranking.
5
Click "Inspect" on a suspicious item to open its detail page, with the full time-series, anomaly markers, and snapshot overlay. That's where you decide: validate the forecast, or push it back?
